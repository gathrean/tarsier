## Naming convention rule for future lessons

When authoring new lesson JSON, follow this rule:

- **Lesson title:** English-first. Use the English meaning of the core vocabulary. Keep it short (2-4 words max for roadmap readability).
- **Lesson subtitle:** Tagalog word + English cultural description. Format: "Tagalog — English context."
- **Exception:** If the Tagalog word has no clean English translation (po, opo, naman, ba), keep the Tagalog as the title.

Examples for future chapters:
- Lesson on "kain": title "Let's Eat!", subtitle "Kain — Food & Eating"
- Lesson on "saan": title "Where?", subtitle "Saan — Asking for Directions"
- Lesson on "ba": title "Ba", subtitle "The Question Particle" *(no English equivalent)*
- Lesson on "maganda": title "Beautiful", subtitle "Maganda — Descriptions & Compliments"
